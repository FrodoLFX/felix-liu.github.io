What I Care About

Systems That Don’t Fail Quietly

I like working on systems where a bug has real consequences: vehicle safety, power systems, embedded control, and verification. These environments force you to think about edge cases, observability, and how tests fail—not just how they pass.

Bridging Firmware & Integration

My work sits between low-level firmware and high-level system behavior. I enjoy reading schematics, debugging signals with oscilloscopes, and then tracing the issues back into software or configuration.

Hands-On Debugging

Whether it’s chasing a CAN signal that only fails at -20°C or tracking a ghost bug on a mainframe batch job, I like the detective work of figuring out why a system behaves unexpectedly.
